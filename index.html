Patents

What I have invented

Eye surgery recovery mask

Having suffered from complications after my eye surgery when I was contaminated by my eyemask, I patented a type of recovery mask which allows patients to redress the wound without taking off the mask to avoid contamination by bacteria in the air. It also reduces swelling and relieves the pain through cold compress and partial massage.

Energy-saving trash bin

It separates liquid and solid garbage automatically, therefore reducing the difficulty of cleaning the trash bin and slowing down corruption to avoid unpleasant smells. Environmentally friendly physical billbaords could be replaced easily instead of electronic billboard. Energy-saving LED displayers are implemented to save energy.

Reconfigurable power strip

As a person who loves travel, I designed an easily portable reconfigurable power strip to keep all my devices charged and ready to use at all times. It is a multi-layer nested power strip, which comprises a four-stage telescopic rod and four layers of power strip with jacks on their sides. It is small and portable compared to conventional power strips in the market. The number of jacks can be changed based on different needs.
